Limiting food waste

Food waste is a huge problem in the US. 40% of the food produced goes uneaten. It's almost half of it! It's sad in a country where 1 out 6 people is food insecure, meaning that 52 millions of Americans are not sure to eat everyday.

How might we, as individuals, optimize food sustainably and seamlessly?

Meet Feedback, a smart food optimizer

Feedback is a smart assistant that will leverage on your home devices to help you do good with your food by first preempting your needs. But because life is also unpredictable, Feedback will figure out the best options to redirect your food if things don't work as planned.
